AquaPure

AquaPure is a microplastics removal concept developed as a group project, focused on improving water quality through sustainable filtration and environmental health considerations.

The project explored how innovative filtration systems could reduce microplastic contamination in water systems while remaining scalable and accessible. My role involved research, problem framing, and contributing to the overall project strategy.
