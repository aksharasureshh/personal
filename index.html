Hi, I’m Akshara! ✨

I’m a Science & Business student at the University of Waterloo — a path I chose somewhat indecisively, but one that turned out to be the best decision I’ve made so far.

Studying both science and business has given me the ability to move between these worlds, and I’ve come to see myself as a translator between them.

I’m most drawn to work that’s tangible and grounded in real life — projects where the outcomes matter to people. Whether it’s making someone’s day a little easier or contributing to work that can save a life, I find meaning in solving problems where the impact is visible and human.

I aim to build things thoughtfully and intentionally — never rushed.
